Madjarica Pizza

Ingredients

  • 20g of fresh yeast
  • 400 g of soft flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 150 ml of warm water
  • About 70 ml of tomato juice
  • Oregano to taste
  • Chees
  • Peperoni
  • mushrooms

Recipe

Put the yeast to rise (mix the yeast with 1 teaspoon of sugar, 2 teaspoons of flour and a little lukewarm water. Cover the bowl with the yeast or cover it with a kitchen towel and leave it for a few minutes). After that, add flour, salt, oil, warm water and knead the dough (the dough should be soft and sticky). Leave the dough in a covered bowl to rest in a warm place for about 30 minutes. Divide it into two parts, for two pizzas, and roll out on baking paper in the shape of a circle. Pour tomato juice on the stretched dough, sprinkle with oregano and fill as desired.And bake it for 10 mins
